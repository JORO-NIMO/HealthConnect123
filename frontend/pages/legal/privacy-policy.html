Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how HealthConnect collects, uses, stores, and shares personal information, including health-related information, when you use our website, mobile web application, and related services.

On this page

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Account data: name, email address, phone number, password hash, profile details, and role selection (patient, doctor, hospital administrator, or admin).
  • Health and medical data you submit: symptoms, consultation notes, prescriptions, vital signs, medical history, uploaded records, and related communications.
  • Appointment and transaction data: appointment schedules, payment status, invoices, and service usage records.
  • Technical and usage data: IP address, device/browser type, log events, pages visited, and service performance information.
  • Support interactions: messages, feedback, and issue reports sent to our support team.

2. How We Use Information

  • To create and manage your account and authenticate access.
  • To provide telemedicine services, including triage, appointments, consultation tools, and health record access.
  • To facilitate communication between patients, doctors, hospitals, and administrators.
  • To process payments and detect fraud, abuse, or unauthorized activity.
  • To improve service quality, security, reliability, and user experience.
  • To comply with legal, regulatory, and clinical record-keeping obligations.

3. Legal Bases for Processing

Depending on your location and applicable laws, we process personal information based on one or more of the following:

  • Your consent (for example, where you choose to submit health information or enable optional features).
  • Performance of a contract (to deliver requested services).
  • Compliance with legal obligations.
  • Legitimate interests, including service improvement, security, and misuse prevention.

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell personal health information. We may share information only as needed with:

  • Licensed healthcare professionals and facilities participating in your care.
  • Service providers who support hosting, notifications, analytics, identity verification, and payments.
  • Regulators, law enforcement, courts, or other authorities when required by law.
  • Emergency responders where disclosure is necessary to prevent serious harm.

All third-party processing is subject to confidentiality and security safeguards appropriate to the nature of the data.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to provide services, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Medical and billing records may be retained for longer periods where required by law or clinical standards.

6. Security

We use administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect your information, including encryption in transit, access controls, authentication measures, logging, and monitoring. No system is perfectly secure, so we encourage users to maintain strong credentials and report suspicious activity promptly.

7. International Transfers

If your information is processed outside your country, we apply safeguards required by applicable law, such as contractual protections and restricted access controls.

8. Your Rights

Subject to local law, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your data, and to request a copy of your personal information. You may also withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To submit a privacy request, contact us at support@healthconnect.health. We may request identity verification before processing requests.

9. Children

Our platform is not intended for independent use by children under the minimum age required by law in their jurisdiction. When child-related care is provided, an authorized parent or legal guardian must create and manage access as required.

10. Cookies and Similar Technologies

HealthConnect primarily uses browser local storage for session continuity and authentication state in the web application. We may still rely on limited, essential technical cookies set by hosting or infrastructure layers for security, routing, or reliability. We do not use advertising cookies on this platform.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through the platform or by other reasonable notice methods. The updated version is effective on the posted date.
