## Privacy Policy

### 1) Information We Collect
We may collect:
- Account data (name, email, phone, role, profile details, password hash).
- Health-related data you submit (symptoms, consultations, prescriptions, records, vitals, messages).
- Appointment and transaction data (bookings, payment status, invoices).
- Technical usage data (IP, device/browser type, logs, pages visited, performance metrics).
- Support communications.

### 2) How We Use Information
We use data to:
- Create and secure accounts.
- Deliver telemedicine services.
- Support communication between patients, doctors, hospitals, and admins.
- Process payments and prevent fraud/abuse.
- Improve reliability, quality, and security.
- Meet legal and regulatory obligations.

### 3) Legal Bases
Depending on local law, processing may rely on:
- Consent.
- Contract performance.
- Legal obligation.
- Legitimate interests (security, quality, platform protection).

### 4) Sharing
We do not sell personal health information.
We may share data only when needed with:
- Participating licensed providers/facilities.
- Service providers (hosting, notifications, analytics, identity verification, payments).
- Authorities where required by law.
- Emergency responders to prevent serious harm.

### 5) Retention
Data is kept only as long as needed for service delivery, compliance, dispute handling, and contract enforcement. Some medical and billing records may be retained longer when required by law.

### 6) Security
HealthConnect applies technical and organizational safeguards such as encryption in transit, access controls, authentication, logging, and monitoring.

### 7) International Transfers
When data crosses borders, safeguards are applied as required by law.

### 8) Your Rights
Subject to local law, you may request access, correction, deletion, restriction, portability, or consent withdrawal.
Contact: support@healthconnect.health

### 9) Children
The platform is not intended for independent use by children under legal minimum age in their jurisdiction.

### 10) Cookies and Similar Technologies
HealthConnect primarily uses browser local storage for authentication/session continuity.
Only limited essential technical cookies may be used by hosting/infrastructure for security/routing/reliability.
No advertising cookies are used.

### 11) Changes
Policies may be updated over time; material changes are communicated appropriately.

### 12) Contact
HealthConnect Privacy Team
Email: support@healthconnect.health

## Terms and Conditions

### 1) Acceptance
Using HealthConnect means you accept these Terms and the Privacy Policy.

### 2) Eligibility and Accounts
- Users must be legally able to enter an agreement.
- Users are responsible for account credentials and activity.
- Information provided must remain accurate.
- Healthcare professionals must provide truthful verification/licensing details.

### 3) Medical Notice
- Platform content and AI outputs support care but are not standalone diagnosis/treatment.
- In emergencies, contact local emergency services immediately.

### 4) Telemedicine Services
- Clinical responsibility remains with qualified professionals.
- Availability may vary by location, provider schedule, and capacity.
- Users consent to electronic communication/records for care delivery.

### 5) Payments and Billing

- Refunds depend on service type, completion status, and law.

### 6) User Obligations

### 9) Availability
HealthConnect aims for reliability but does not guarantee uninterrupted or error-free service.

### 10) Limitation of Liability
To the fullest extent allowed by law, indirect/consequential damages are excluded and direct liability is limited as allowed by applicable law.

### 11) Suspension/Termination

### 12) Governing Law and Disputes

### 13) Changes to Terms

## Medical Disclaimer

- HealthConnect provides digital health workflows and AI-assisted informational support.
- Platform information is not, by itself, a medical diagnosis or treatment plan.
- Medical decisions must be made by qualified healthcare professionals.
- Do not delay seeking medical advice based on platform information.
- For emergencies, contact local emergency services immediately.
- Outcomes, provider availability, and response times may vary.
- Use of platform implies understanding of telemedicine limitations and need for accurate user-provided health information.
